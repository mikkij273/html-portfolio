My Favorite Movies of All Time

My List of Some of the Greats

ET

A sweet comical sci-fi movie, of an alien that just wants to get back home.

The Color Purple

A beautiful coming of age story, of an opressed young black woman, that must find her voice and her purpose.

The Help

Set in a time when the South was very unkind to blacks, partcularly for black domestic help women.

John Wick

Action! Filled with rage after being disrespected from some young punks that stole his car,
John Wick enters back into the underworld he thought he left behind, he only wanted to be left alone to mourn the loss of his wife.

Home Alone

Classic comedy. Two would be burgulars have met there match, when they decide to target the house of Kevin McCallister, who is home alone.
Two stupid criminals, one young kid wise beyond his years, and a house full of everything you need to kick their butts.
